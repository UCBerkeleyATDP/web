The final challenge of 2013 is part puzzle, part story, and part game!<\/p>\n

An evil wizard has locked the princess in the dungeon. Luckily, she had a hairpin, and used it to pick the lock and escape from her cell.\u00a0 Now, she\u2019s trying to make her way out of the dungeon. (See a static map of the dungeon below<\/a> or an interactive one here<\/a>!)<\/p>\n

The princess starts at the squared labeled P, on the 1st<\/sup> floor.<\/p>\n

The goal is to get the ladder on the 6th<\/sup> floor. It\u2019s the square marked with the L.<\/p>\n

The princess can move up, down, left, or right. She can\u2019t move diagonally.<\/p>\n

Since the dungeon has no stairs or elevators (or escalators), the princess has to use the Warp Squares to get around. The Warp Squares come in numbered pairs. The princess can warp between these pairs. So, if the princess is on the square marked W1 on the 1st<\/sup> floor, she can warp to the square marked W1 on 4th<\/sup> floor. A warp counts as one move.<\/p>\n

The dungeon also has a few hazards. The black squares are black holes and if the princess moves into one, she will be crushed.<\/p>\n

There are also several bears. The princess is nimble, but she can only move so fast. If she ever occupies the same square as a bear, she will get eaten up. She can, however, safely slip past a bear as long as they aren\u2019t on the same square.<\/p>\n

Every time the princess moves (up, down, left, right, or warp), the bears also move.\u00a0 Each bear loops through a set movement pattern. \u00a0The bear will repeat the same movement pattern over and over, from the beginning. The movement patterns (figured out by the princess) are listed below:<\/p>\n

Bear 1: Up, Right, Down, Left<\/p>\n

Bear 2: Up, Left, Down, Left, Right, Right<\/p>\n

Bear 3: Left, Left, Right, Right<\/p>\n

Bear 4: Up, Right, Down, Left<\/p>\n

Bear 5: Right, Up, Up, Up, Left, Down, Down, Down<\/p>\n

Bear 6: Right, Right, Right, Right, Right, Left, Left, Left, Left, Left<\/p>\n

Bear 7: Up, Up, Right, Right, Right, Down, Down, Left, Left, Left<\/p>\n

Bear 8: Left, Right
