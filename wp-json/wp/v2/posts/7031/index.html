Have you ever had an idea for your own scientific investigation? The following release from the National Association of Gifted Children (NAGC) outlines an exciting opportunity for innovative students in scientific fields. Check it out!<\/p>\n

The Johns Hopkins Center for Talented Youth (CTY) and Cogito have established the\u00a0CTY Cogito Research Awards<\/a>\u00a0to enable bright middle- and high-school students to conduct research in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) fields. A committee composed of Johns Hopkins CTY faculty will select students who submit the most compelling, promising research proposals. Up to 10 award winners will be chosen this inaugural year to receive grants to help cover expenses associated with their research projects and will be paired with a mentor to support them through the research process.<\/p>\n

Proposal submission deadline is\u00a0Oct. 31, so please share with your students and families.\n<\/p><\/blockquote>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
