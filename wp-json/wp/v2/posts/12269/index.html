Returning student priority deadline: February 17<\/h4>\n

Applicants who have completed a Secondary Division class before will receive priority consideration if they postmark their complete applications by February 17. After this date, all SD applications are considered in the order completed.<\/p>\n

New student & Financial aid deadline: March 2<\/h4>\n

New applicants and those who have previously completed only an Elementary Division class should postmark their applications by March 2. Requests for need-based financial aid must<\/strong><\/em> be postmarked by this date.<\/p>\n

Late applications accepted<\/h4>\n

Applications received after March 2 are accepted on a space-available basis. As before, they will be considered in the order completed, and financial aid requests will no longer be accepted.<\/p>\n
