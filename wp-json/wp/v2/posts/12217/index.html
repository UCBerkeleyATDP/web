Secondary Division courses<\/h3>\n

Advanced Reading & Writing<\/h4>\n

The study of language broadens horizons and sharpens the mind. This course is for students completing 10th and 11th grades who love to read and write and would like to amplify their skills in a vibrant, intensive setting.

Fundamentals of Art<\/h4>\n

This hands-on studio course will provide a comprehensive, in-depth study of the fundamentals of the arts. Media will include pencil, ink, charcoal, pastel, watercolors, tempera paint, and collage.

Introduction to Python (Beginning Programming)<\/h4>\n

This brand new semester-equivalent course is designed for students who are new to programming.

Elementary Division<\/h3>\n

The Baker Street Irregulars<\/h4>\n

The popular Baker Street Irregulars is coming back after a hiatus! This is a great class for students who enjoy the challenge of solving intriguing problems that test their thinking. Students completing 5th and 6th grades may apply.
