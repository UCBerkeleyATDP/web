<\/h1>\n

This past spring, ATDP lost one of its best friends when Dr. Gary A. Griffin passed away. Dr. Griffin was an internationally respected educator, former dean at the University of Illinois, professor emeritus at Teachers College, Columbia University, and passionate advocate of rich learning for all children. Upon his passing, his sister Dena Griffin requested that her brother’s friends and colleagues honor his memory through a donation to ATDP. On behalf of the students whose attendance at ATDP was made possible through the kindess and consideration of Dena Griffin and Dr. Griffin’s many friends, we give our deepest thanks.<\/p>\n
