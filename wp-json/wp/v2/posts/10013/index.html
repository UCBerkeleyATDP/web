ATDP will be having an information session for parents and students interested in learning more about ATDP’s Secondary Division on the UC Berkeley campus for summer 2015. Dr. Frank C. Worrell, ATDP’s Faculty Director, will give a presentation, and you will have an opportunity to ask questions. <\/p>\n

Students who will have completed seventh grade by June 2015 should apply to the Secondary Division. Returning students who will be completing sixth grade in June 2015 may apply to the Elementary Division, but also have the option of applying for a few Secondary Division courses. These students must be ready for the challenge of demanding, rigorous coursework. The Director will determine admission and placement.<\/p>\n
