The following additions have been made to the Secondary Division catalog and are now open to applications.<\/p>\n

    \n
  • \nComputer Science A (AP-aligned)<\/strong><\/a> – PM section added
    \nOnline · Completion of Algebra I or Integrated Math 1 required. Completion of an introductory computer science course recommended.<\/strong>
    \nFor students who want to master object-oriented programming. Coding experience recommended but not required. Covers the majority of topics in AP Computer Science A<\/em> in six weeks. With additional self-study and review, students will be prepared to take the AP exam in May. This course was previously titled Programming in Java.<\/em><\/p>\n<\/li>\n
  • \nCognitive Neuroscience<\/strong><\/a> – Course added
    \nOn-site (UC Berkeley campus) · Completion of high school Chemistry, or Biology, or AP Psychology. Applicants must include a report card showing this prerequisite.<\/strong>
    \nFor advanced high school students with a strong science background and a keen interest in the human brain. Students study college-level primary source material.<\/p>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
