All Secondary Division applicants who applied by the postmark deadline have had their placement decisions put in the mail on Thursday, March 27, and those students should expect to receive their letter within the following week! For those who have accounts on our website, your placement information may also be available in your Enrollment menu<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Please do not call the ATDP office regarding your placement status; we cannot release placement information over the phone.<\/strong><\/em> All inquiries regarding placement decisions must be sent to our office in writing. Our email address is atdpoffice@berkeley.edu.<\/p>\n

Those admitted to the 2014 Secondary Division will receive acceptance packets full of important information and instructions to confirm your enrollment. New this year: T-shirts are included<\/a> for each attending student. Admitted students may also use their online Enrollment menu<\/a> to view their placement details, update emergency information, and register for the carpool directory. Remember, the tuition deadline to send in your notification of attendance or withdrawal is Monday, April 28!<\/em><\/p>\n
