The following additions have been made to the Secondary Division catalog and are now open to applications.<\/p>\n

We have added one new course, back from its pandemic hiatus:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • \nIntroduction to Biotechnology<\/strong><\/a> – New course; TuTh, AM or PM
    \nOn-site (UC Berkeley campus) · For students completing grades 7 or 8 only.<\/strong>
    \nFor current middle schoolers preparing for high school biology. Introduces concepts through hands-on lab work and modern research technologies. This course takes place in a campus lab and requires strict adherence to safety procedures.<\/p>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Due to high demand, we have added a second section to the following courses:<\/p>\n

      \n
    • \nIntroduction to Public Speaking<\/strong><\/a> – WF PM section added
      \nOn-site (UC Berkeley campus) · For students completing grades 6–9.<\/strong>
      \nFor students who want to develop writing and speaking skills. Covers a variety of speaking styles, oratory, and debate.<\/p>\n<\/li>\n
    • \nIntroduction to Computer Science: Solving Problems with Python<\/strong><\/a> – MWF AM section added
      \nOn-site (UC Berkeley campus) · Open to all qualified SD students.<\/strong>
      \nFor students new to coding with no computer science experience. Covers fundamental concepts in a semester-equivalent programming course. Note:<\/strong> This course has a compressed four-week schedule, beginning June 26 and ending July 21.<\/em><\/p>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
