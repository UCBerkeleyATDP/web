\nBy Tiffany Huynh<\/p>\n

Against the
\nBumpy cream wall of
\nMy bedroom,
\nYou are the full moon that
\nGlows against

\nWarmth of the sun.<\/p>\n

Ode to the Red MP3<\/strong><\/em>
\nNia Jones<\/p>\n

Hear you playing
\nLike an orchestra.
\nYour strings, bass, percussion
\nIn each and every song come together
\nJust profoundly.
\nLike a great, big, musical family reunion.<\/p>\n

\nJoshua Morgan<\/p>\n

\nAlong the shore,
\nI saw you
\nA stone whiter than sun-bleached bone and as smooth as the crystal
\nSky,
\nFloating in a sea of others.

Ode to my Racket<\/strong><\/em>
\nVanessa Van Pell<\/p>\n

You are
\nNervous
\nYour strings tighten
\nAs I warm up<\/p>\n

I feel the blood start to pump
\nThrough every vein in my body
\nYou start to settle
\nKnowing I will protect you<\/p>\n

Ode to a Blade of Grass<\/strong><\/em>
\nBy Vasco Villas-Boas<\/p>\n

I see you carefully tilting in the breeze
\nYou want to slide out of your roots (that won\u2019t give in) and be guided by the wind
\nAnd
\nBe free like a leaf floating downwards from a tree
\nYou are like a
\nHuman
\nThat wants to
\nJump out of a helicopter
\nAnd parachute down
\nTo see the
\nWorld<\/p>\n

Ode to a Tree<\/strong><\/em>
\nEric Yu<\/p>\n

Amid the forest lies you, a tree,
\nA bridge to life
\nThat is a living organism,
\nBut your seeds will sprout into
\nThe new generations of trees ahead of you.
\nThey will feel the same
\nFeeling of luxurious sunlight that you have felt.<\/p>\n
