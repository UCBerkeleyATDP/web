Come one, Come all!<\/h2>\n

Bring a picnic blanket, beach ball, wiffle ball and bat, guitar, or anything else you want to share and enjoy as you mingle with ATDP faculty, staff, and students and families from the Elementary and Secondary Divisions.<\/p>\n

2013 ATDP Picnic<\/strong>
\nSaturday, July 20
\n12:00 noon
\nTolman Hall<\/h4>\n

The ATDP picnic is a potluck, where all attendees contribute to the menu. Be sure to bring plenty of food, according to the first letter of your last name:<\/p>\n

A-K: Main dish<\/strong>
\n(e.g. chicken, pizza, casserole, tacos)<\/p>\n

L-P: Drinks<\/strong>
\n(e.g. preferably canned)<\/p>\n

Q-S: Salad or appetizers<\/strong>
\n(e.g. finger food, salad)<\/p>\n

T-Z: Dessert<\/strong>
\n(e.g. cookies, cupcakes, fruit)<\/p>\n

Additional Picnic Events:<\/h4>\n
    \n
  • SD Information Session (11:00, 2326 Tolman Hall)<\/li>\n
  • Student Art Exhibition & Slideshow (12:00, 2523 Tolman Hall)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
