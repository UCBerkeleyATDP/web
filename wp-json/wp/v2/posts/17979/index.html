    Application season set to start mid-January<\/h3>\n

    ATDP normally publishes its course catalogs and opens applications over the winter break. The past two years upended our usual timelines, and while we were hoping to return to our usual calendar, continued uncertainties and evolving University policies have required a little more time to plan! We are aiming to have our application process open to the public around mid-January of 2022.<\/p>\n

    We highly recommend subscribing to our community newsletter<\/a> if you haven’t already! We send very infrequent updates (2 or 3 a year) via email, including for the opening of our applications.<\/p>\n

    Faculty positions now open<\/h3>\n

    ATDP is always on the lookout for amazing teachers to join our summer cohort. We still have a handful of spots to fill for currently planned 2022 courses, and we always welcome new course proposals.
