    ATDP’s Global Programs<\/strong>, administered through UC Berkeley\u2019s Graduate School of Education, offers challenging courses to highly motivated middle and high school international students. International students have the option of applying for courses as individuals through the Elementary Division<\/a> or Secondary Division<\/a>.<\/p>\n

    Alternatively, ATDP can design a program to meet the needs of a group of international students, like the program that we have designed for students from Singapore Chinese Girls\u2019 School (SCGS). Our largest international partnership is a program for middle and high school students from China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and India (ASSET<\/a>) in association with CFM Educational Services:<\/p>\n
