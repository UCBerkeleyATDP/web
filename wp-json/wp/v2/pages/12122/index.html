Additional Option for Returning Sixth Graders<\/h2>\n

Returning ED students who will be completing sixth grade in June 2023 are also eligible to apply for the following Secondary Division (SD) courses:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • The Writing Process<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Introduction to Public Speaking<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Elements of Web Design<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Foundations of Algebra<\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Spaces in these six-week courses are reserved for exceptional returning students who are ready for the challenge of demanding, rigorous coursework. The Director will determine admission and placement. Students who decide to apply to SD must complete an SD application. Former ED students applying to SD are considered new students for application purposes. The SD early application deadline is Wednesday, February 15 (recommended), and the standard deadline is Wednesday, March 15.<\/p>\n

    Since placement in SD is not guaranteed for returning sixth graders, a sixth grade SD applicant should also consider acceptable ED course choices as alternatives.
